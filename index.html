Why Clean Monuments?

Cleaning monuments offers many advantages. Find out more information below!

Preservation

Over time monuments often have mildew, mold, moss, and other biological matter grow on it. The stone will become to look neglected. Perhaps even more important, the stone may become permantly stained and un-restorable.

Respect

Grave sites are often the final resting place. It is important to show respect to loved ones. If left unmaintained, the stone may become unreadable. It is important to keep clean so future generations can pay respect to ancestors.

Professional Services

Many people belive they can simply power wash the stone, but doing so is incredibly dangerous. The high pressure often causes more harm than good. It is common to get chips, breakages, and other damage to the stone as a result.

General Cleaning

Using a custom cleaning product, we will cautiously and carefully clean the stone removing moss, mildew, dirt, and other debris from the stone. General Cleaning and Site Maintenance

This package provides all of the General Cleaning, in addition to general maintenance around the site. We will trim the grass, bushes, or trees surrounding the site. We will pay attention to every detail of the entire site. Can be single time or annually.
