Frequently Asked Questions

Below are common questions we are asked. We are more than happy to elaborate and answer any other questions you may have!

What areas do you service?

We service a 30 mile radius in Pittsburgh. We can make special accomidations in certain cases. Contact us to discuss possible options.

What types of headstones can be cleaned?

We service most Granite and Marble stones. Bronze stones can also be cleaned. Most headstones can be cleaned.

I don't know the exact stone type, can you still clean it?

We will be able to determine the type during our FREE estimate. No worries if you don't know!

Why should I have my monument cleaned?

Stains on your monument can become permanent over time. Cleaning the stone will remove mosses, mildew, and several other types of organic material from the stone.

Will cleaning the stone damage it?

We will not damage your stone. During our Free estimate, we will use our judgement on the cleaning process. We will clean the stone to the best of our ability without damaging it.

What do you use to clean?

We use a combination of water, granite shiner, a specialized cleaning solution (safe for the Earth), and an assortment of specially made brushes

I still have questions, what do I do?

Contact us! Feel free to call (610) 350-1316 or use our form on the website.

Our Promise

We guarantee complete satisfaction or your money back! We pride ourselves in taking the utmost care of each stone. We use specialized products to ensure the integrity of the stone. There are certain situations where they cannot be cleaned to absolute perfection.
