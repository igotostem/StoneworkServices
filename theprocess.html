The Process

Below is a video and explanation of how we clean each stone with care.

The process starts by working with you to determine exactly what you want. We will figure out the exact scope of each project.

Upon arrival in the cemetary, we will carefully remove overgrown grass around the base of the stone. We will brush off any lose debris, such as dust, grass, and leaves.

After the preparation stages, we begin to clean the stone. We use a special product which is formulated especially for monuments. This product is non-toxic, biodegradable, and is safe around wildlife. We never use bleaches, ammonia, acids, or other harsh chemicals which can wear the stone. The product is sprayed on the entire stone surface.

We then use an assortment of brushes to scrub the entire stone. We have special brushes which allow us to get into the engraving of the stone. Every letter and design are carefully cleaned. We ensure that we are not too rough on the stone to prevent any chips, scratches, or other faults.

After the actual cleaning, we will rinse the entire stone. We will then use a different cleaning product to wipe the polished surfaces of the stone to reveal a nice shiny surface.

We take extreme caution into the entire process. We have many steps in line to keep every stone in immaculate shape.
